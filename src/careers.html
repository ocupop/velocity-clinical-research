Capabilities

Ensuring Enrollment Goals with an Integrated Approach

Achieving timely study start-up, patient recruitment, and retaining patients to obtain all study data remain key challenges for Sponsors and CROs. Velocity Clinical Research provides a consistent, scalable approach to tackling these challenges. By offering access to our wholly owned research sites and providing central support and contacts for many operational activities, we make your job easier.

We are committed to rapid responsiveness from initial feasibility and contracting to patient enrollment and study close-out. Our uniform cross-site processes are designed to deliver patients and data as quickly as possible without sacrificing exemplary data quality and patient care.

Velocity sites prioritize positive patient experiences and outcomes and we work hard to deliver outstanding patient care. Engaging patients and creating an environment of comfort, communication and medical care enhances our ability to retain patients and to elicit referrals for future studies.

Our company can help you achieve your patient enrollment goals with the assurances of high quality data and an experienced team.
